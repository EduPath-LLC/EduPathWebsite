Welcome To Our Prototype!

Edupath is an initiative started by high school students in order to mitigate students' confusion when navigating buildings.

Features

- Map


Our application offers schools an easy way to navigate campuses without tracking location or gathering data. Here at Edupath, we keep people's privacy to a high standard.


- Security


Edupath provides users with optimal security. Login data is protected by Google's firebase service.


- Schedule


Edupath allows students to access the maps to their classrooms according to their schedules easily and effectively.


- Schedule Alerts


Edupath has the ability to alert students if it is an A/B day and if their class starts soon.
